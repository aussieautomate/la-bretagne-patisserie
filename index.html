Our Story

A French tradition,
crafted in Sydney.

For over two decades, La Bretagne Patisserie has been bringing the finest traditions of French baking to the beautiful harbourside suburb of Rose Bay. Every morning our pastry chefs begin work before dawn, ensuring that what lands in your hands is as fresh and exquisite as it would be on a Parisian street corner.

From flaky croissants and rustic sourdoughs to delicate macarons and show-stopping celebration cakes — everything is made in-house with premium ingredients and genuine French technique.

Our Celebrated Creations

Signature Cakes

Each cake is a labour of love — available to order for birthdays, weddings, and every occasion worth celebrating.

La Concorde

A classic French meringue and chocolate mousse masterpiece — airy, indulgent, and utterly elegant.

Baked Cheesecake

Rich, velvety and perfectly golden — our house-baked cheesecake is a perennial favourite.

Black Forest Gâteau

Layers of dark chocolate, kirsch-soaked cherries and fresh cream — a timeless German-French icon.

Tiramisù with Candied Almonds

Our signature twist on the Italian classic — espresso-soaked sponge crowned with crunchy candied almonds.

Tarte au Citron

Bright, silky lemon curd in a buttery shortcrust shell — a quintessential French patisserie classic.

Custom Celebration Cakes
